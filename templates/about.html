About Us

GymEncore simplifies gym operations by integrating membership management, instructor coordination, subscriptions, payments, and attendance tracking. Our platform is designed to empower gyms with seamless digital solutions, ensuring members and instructors enjoy a smooth and connected experience.

Our Location

GymEncore is proudly based at Encore Gym Fitness Studio in Buruburu, Nairobi.

Mumias South Road, Nairobi
Our Mission

To revolutionize fitness management by providing a user-friendly system that enhances member engagement, supports instructors, and streamlines administrative tasks. We aim to make fitness accessible, organized, and motivating for everyone involved.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital platform for gyms and fitness centers across Africa and beyond, fostering healthier communities through technology-driven fitness solutions and sustainable growth.
